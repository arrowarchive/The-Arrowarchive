Calling Out Mayday

My first drawing of the sky rabbits featured Nimbus and her evolution line, but after listening to “Mayday” by TheFatRat, I was inspired to draw her again.

This time, it’s a yin and yang-like drawing featuring Nimbus and her counterpart, Mayonaka (真夜中), whose names translates to midnight in Japanese. She’s a similar species of rabbit, but with different powers compared to Nimbus. I like her design, so I need to draw her again later.

That said, I love how the midnight/sunrise contrast on this piece turned out. It took a while, but I’m very proud of it!

September 28th, 2020
