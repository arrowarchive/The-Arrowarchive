Dryya Concept

Yes, this character has the same name as a character in Hollow Knight. Moving on!

I was stuck in an art block for a while; I finished a birthday gift, I made some original drawings, and I've been updating this website. Once August began, I couldn't come up with anything new.

I wanted to practice with perspective, rooms, and furniture, but nothing looked right and I grew frustrated because of it. I was getting better with posing and I had good references to work with, but I wasn't inspired.

I worked out of the block by writing and researching interior design, but I wanted to draw something new. I've drawn a lot of SPACE characters and I couldn't think of anything for the others, so I drew a second moth character, much like Cithera.

Her name is Dryya and she's based on the Rosy Maple moth. I was inspired to draw her after talking with someone on Discord, and I had pictures of the moth in another tab since May before I got to work.

I had a feeling that a Rosy Maple Moth would have a sweet personality to match its appearance. I made Dryya look like a pop star and she was supposed to have a microphone, but I couldn't fit it in without covering her face. Serendipity writes the plot, I guess.

I plan to revisit this character later. I didn't shade her or try anything too fancy because I was more focused on working through my art block. I may redesign her as a result, but I'm not worried about that right now. For now, I want to see if I can experiment with furniture/perspective/backgrounds a little more. Maybe I can actually pull it off this time.

August 7th, 2021
