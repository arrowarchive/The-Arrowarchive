2020 Pinterest Studies

A new layout is planned for entries with multiple pictures. If this entry goes down at a future date or the link redirects elsewhere, that's why.

These were previously undated drawings I posted on Instagram in 2020. I have found when I posted them, so I am archiving them here. As a result, these are my present thoughts on the drawings.

I was not confident in my artistic abilities when I drew these. Pinterest was my main outlet for pose references, and even then, I struggled with proportions. I remember thinking about changing Chlora's hair around this time, as her previous lost treasure has a different hair style. That, and her star is on her tie instead of a pin. As for Aqua? she hasn't changed much.

I have a shadow drawing from time period that was a part of the pose study, but I grouped her drawing with an Electra drawing instead because the Electra drawing was undated and both of their pose references were from Danganronpa games. Hence, she has a different entry.

I stopped using Pinterest a few months after completing these. Why? because I couldn't find poses and clothing designs that were free to use. A lot of references I saved were from Deviantart, Wattpad, Pixiv, etc. that were likely posted without the artist's permission. I found that some of the group photo references I liked the most were originally from accounts that didn't want people using them. I drew the line when an outfit reference I saved was taken down because the person who reposted it infringed the artist's copyright.

Moral of the story? Check the description of a post/the artist's terms of service before picking a reference. As for the artist who designed the clothes? I now follow them on Twitter.

May 4th, 2020
