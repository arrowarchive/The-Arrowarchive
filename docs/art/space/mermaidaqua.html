Mermaid Aqua

I love the mermaid set in Animal Crossing: New Horizons, so when I learned that the developers added clothes to the series, I was ecstatic! After obtaining a few of the items, I looked up what the rest of them looked like, and to my surprise, there was a pink palette!

I wanted to draw one of my characters wearing the outfits, but I couldn’t decide on the colors. Ultimately, due to Aqua’s palette, I made her wear the pink clothes. This was fun to work on, and I don’t draw my characters wearing fun outfits as much as I should. Maybe I should fix that.

July 20th, 2020
